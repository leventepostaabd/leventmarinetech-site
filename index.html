Materials Desk

We find the part the OEM no longer stocks.

CN/JP/KR-built older bulker switchgear, PLC cards, AVR modules, sensors — items no longer in the OEM catalog. Through our sourcing network we locate them and ship to the vessel; when they cannot be found, we propose a compatible replacement.

  • AOG / Emergency Global courier delivery direct to the vessel within 24 hours — for critical trip-out faults.
  • Obsolete spares Second-source / refurbished sourcing for legacy Mitsubishi, Hitachi, Hyundai, Stork, Terasaki gear.
  • Compatible alternative When the original isn't available, we recommend a technically compatible modern equivalent with an engineering note.
  • Bulk materials Cable, terminals, fuses, contactors — marine-grade material lists for the ship's project store.
