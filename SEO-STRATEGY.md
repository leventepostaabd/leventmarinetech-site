## 5. On-Page SEO Checklist

- [ ] **Title tag:** 50-60 characters, keyword-rich
- [ ] **Meta description:** 150-160 characters, action-oriented CTA
- [ ] **H1:** Main keyword (once)
- [ ] **H2/H3:** Subheadings with secondary keywords
- [ ] **Image alt text:** Descriptive, 8-12 words
- [ ] **Internal links:** 3-5 relevant posts minimum
- [ ] **External links:** 2-3 authoritative sources (class standards, SOLAS)
- [ ] **Schema.org markup:** Article JSON-LD (already done)
- [ ] **Word count:** 1000-1500 words (evergreen content)
- [ ] **Readability:** Flesch-Kincaid 8-12 grade level (technical audience OK)
- [ ] **Keywords:** Primary keyword 1-2%, secondary keywords 0.5-1% (natural)
- [ ] **CTA:** Clear call-to-action (service link + contact form)
